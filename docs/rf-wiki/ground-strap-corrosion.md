Ground strap corrosion can raise ground impedance and create desense, PIM, or unstable OTA behavior after humidity, sweat, salt, or THB stress. Look for surface discoloration, contact resistance increase, pressure sensitivity, and improvement after cleaning or strap replacement.

The RCA should separate corrosion evidence from ordinary assembly variation. Include environmental stress history, contact resistance measurement, visual inspection, and replacement A/B.
