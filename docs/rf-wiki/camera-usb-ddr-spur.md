Camera, USB, or DDR activity can create narrow spurs or broadband coupling depending on clock, data burst, cable, and ground return path. Use function ON/OFF, throughput or frame-rate sweep, near-field scan, and shielding A/B to isolate the source.

Document the active function, traffic condition, spur frequency, and whether the fail is reproduced in conducted, OTA, or both paths.
