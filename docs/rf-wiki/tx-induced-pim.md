Tx-correlated desense with shield, clip, spring, screw, bracket, back glass, or other contact clues should keep a PIM or contact nonlinearity hypothesis alive until disproved. Useful separation tests include Tx power sweep, pressure A/B, reassembly A/B, contact cleaning or insulation, and IM3/IM5 frequency checks.

Do not conclude PIM from structure clues alone. The decision needs correlation with Tx power, channel, intermodulation product, or repeatable mechanical sensitivity.
