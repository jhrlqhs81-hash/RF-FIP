Chamber or fixture validation is required when OTA fail is marginal, not repeatable, fixture dependent, or inconsistent with conducted baseline and field evidence. Compare golden unit, fixture swap, cable check, calibration state, chamber repeatability, orientation, and retest sequence.

Do not promote a root cause from a single OTA fail until measurement repeatability and fixture influence are separated from device behavior.
