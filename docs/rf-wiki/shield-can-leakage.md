Shield can leakage or weak grounding is suspected when near-field scan hotspots, shielding A/B improvement, or pressure-sensitive shield edge behavior correlate with desense. Check shield contact continuity, solder cracks, lid deformation, ground spring contact, and whether a temporary absorber or copper tape changes the spur or noise floor.

Shielding improvement is evidence for coupling path isolation, not by itself a root cause. The RCA should still name the source, coupling path, and repeatable A/B result.
