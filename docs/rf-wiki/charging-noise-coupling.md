Charging noise coupling is suspected when desense appears only with charger attached, specific current level, cable orientation, or charge pump mode. Compare charger ON/OFF, current sweep, cable and adapter A/B, conducted baseline, OTA baseline, and near-field scan around charging and power paths.

Avoid naming charger as root cause without separating adapter, cable, board power path, and RF coupling mechanism.
