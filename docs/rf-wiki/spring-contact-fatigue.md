Spring contact fatigue can create intermittent ground or antenna feed behavior after drop, repeated assembly, compression set, or plating contamination. Clues include pressure A/B sensitivity, reassembly recovery, lot or cycle dependency, and inconsistent OTA or PIM behavior.

Useful checks include contact height measurement, force gauge comparison, microscope inspection, cleaning A/B, replacement A/B, and repeatability after thermal or mechanical stress.
