Back glass assembly can affect antenna loading, ground contact, or pressure-sensitive structures. Clues include OTA improvement under localized pressure, fail disappearance after reassembly, adhesive thickness variation, conductive decoration proximity, or changed gap to antenna structures.

Confirm the press location, assembly stack, adhesive or tape condition, repeatability, and whether conducted RX remains stable. Do not treat reassembly recovery alone as final mitigation.
