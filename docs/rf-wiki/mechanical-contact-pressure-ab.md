Pressure-sensitive RF degradation points to mechanical contact, grounding, antenna feed, shield can, spring contact, or back glass assembly conditions. A pressure A/B test should record press location, direction, force condition, repeatability, and whether the result affects conducted or OTA performance.

Reassembly disappearance is useful evidence but can hide root cause. Treat it as a clue for contact or assembly sensitivity, not a final corrective action.
