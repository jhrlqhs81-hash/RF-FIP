FPC contact intermittency should be considered when RF performance changes with connector reseat, bending, flex pressure, or assembly order. The issue may appear as unstable conducted power, intermittent sensitivity, antenna path loss, or noise coupling depending on which FPC path is affected.

Record reseat A/B, bend A/B, connector inspection, latch condition, contamination, and whether the failure is reproducible after controlled assembly.
